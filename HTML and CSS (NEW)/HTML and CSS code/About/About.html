Problem Statement:

Cancer is a disease that often goes unnoticed till its too late. By the time cancer is finally detected it is at a fatal stage that almost certainly results in death. Although cancer treatment is slowly improving, the doctor to patient ratio is 1:1000 in India. T his shows that majority of patients do not get any treatment or even diagnosis at all. The lack of doctors and treatment for the general public is not only due to the scarcity of treatment but also due to the fact that most people can not afford the full cost for treatments let alone diagnosis. By creating a decentralized solution we allow for anyone and everyone to access a diagnosis at any point.


About:

This application uses machine learning as well as a regressive model to correlate data related to other cancer patient sample, the model looks at various factors like symptoms, age, weight, bad habits to name a few. By comparing the client's inputted symptoms to those of ten thousand other patients , we give you a numerical probability aimed to predict whether you should be cautious of Cancer - And based on this probability the program puts you in low risk, medium risk or high risk of Cancer. Upon falling into any one of these categories, it redirects the user to ways they can decrease their risk.


Usage:

Hence, our application helps raise awareness while also simultaneously helping assess the risk of a person getting cancer and what level of risk they are at. To use this app simply fill out the questionnaire and also submit a sample from your genome as well as a medical report from a checkup, or simply scan the report sheet via your phone.


Disclaimer:

This application is not 100% accurate and it's results should not be taken as absolute truth. The data may result in conclusions and diagnosis that is outsized, hence take the data with a word of caution. Please always consult with a medical professional before taking any further action or prescribing yourself.
