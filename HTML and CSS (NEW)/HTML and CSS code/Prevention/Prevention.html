What can you do to better your odds?



After going through tens of thousands of survey results, we found some conditions and traits with a high correlation to a cancer positive result :

(In order of largest to smallest correlation from our heatmap data)


1. Smoking
2. Consumption of Alcohol
3. Swallowing difficulty
4. Wheezing
5. Coughing
6. Social and Peer Pressure
7. Chest Pain
8. Fatigue
9. Anxiety
10. Chronic diseases
11. Difficulty breathing




How to avoid smoking?

1. Procrastinate. Push yourself to your limits.
2. Seek therapy (if needed)
3. Chew on it
4. Call healthline



How to prevent alcohol consumption

1. Call helpline
2. Choose not to consume it as much
3. Even when consumed ensure two drinks or less for men and one drink for woman
4. Talk with healthcare provider
5. Keep yourself distracted from consuming alcohol. (example getting hobbies)



How to prevent swallowing difficulty

1.Swallowing exercises can increase strength, mobility, and control of these muscles. Over time, this may help you to swallow normally again.
2.A speech-language pathologist (SLP) may prescribe specific swallowing exercises to improve your swallowing.



How to prevent wheezing


1. Make sure to be in clean and moisturized air
2. Drink more warm liquids
3. Avoid smoking



How to prevent coughing

1. Drink more warm liquids (alongside with honey)
2. Take warm showers
3. Make sure to be in clean and moisturized air
4. Avoid smoking



How to avoid or reduce peer pressure

1. Talk to the individual/inviduals who are pressuring you and let them know how you are feeling because of their actions.
2. Tell a trusted adult (example school teacher, parents)
3. Remember “no” means no



How to reduce chest pain

1. Drink warm liquids
2. Eat ginger regularly
3. Take a sufficient amount of rest
4. Exercise



How to reduce fatigueness?

1.Eat more meals
2.Ensure to get sufficient amount of exercise
3. Lose weight
4. Sleep well
5. Reduce stress
6. Drink more water



Tips to reduce anxiety

1.Deep breathing
2. Journal
3.Exercise
4. Meditate
5.socialize



How to prevent chronic disease

1. Eat healthy foods
2. Get sufficient sleep
3. Exercise



How to improve breathing

1. Practice simple deep breathing exercises
2. Exercise regularly
3. Improve posture
