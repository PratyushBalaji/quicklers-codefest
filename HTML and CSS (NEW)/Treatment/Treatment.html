How to beat your cancer



Below are a few ways to treat and/or slow down lung cancer, prostate cancer, and melanoma.

1.Lung Cancer Treatments
2.Prostate Cancer Treatments
3.Melanoma Treatments


Lung cancer treatment

1. Surgery- A procedure in which doctors cut out and remove the cancer tissue
2. Chemotherapy- uses medications in order to shrink and kill cancer.
(can either be in the form of pills or medication given in the veins)
3. Radiation therapy- Uses high-energy rays to eradicate cancer
4. Targeted therapy- Uses medication to stop the development and spread of cancer cells.



Prostate cancer treatment

1. Surgery- A procedure in which doctors cut out and remove the cancer tissue
2. Chemotherapy- uses medications in order to shrink and kill cancer.
(can either be in the form of pills or medication given in the veins)
3. Radiation therapy- Uses high-energy rays to eradicate cancer
4. Laser Focal Therapy (LST) therapy- can also be used to help improve the symptoms associated with benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH).

Melanoma treatment

1. Surgery- Tumor is removed. (removed until no more cancer cells are seen)
2. Radiation therapy- Uses high-energy rays to eradicate cancer
3. Chemotherapy- uses medications in order to shrink and kill cancer.
4. Photodynamic therapy- laser light is shined onto the skin and a drug becomes active and kills the cancer cells.
5. Immunotherapy- uses the patient's immune system to fight cancer. (restores the body's natural defenses against cancer)
6.Targeted therapy- identifies and kills particular cancer cells using medicines or other chemicals.
7. Chemical peel- The top layers of skin cells are removed from the skin by applying a chemical solution to it.
